Make Some LuluLemonade (Bag Not Included)

August 19, 2019 Emily Powell

4 minute read

 Twitter

 Facebook

 Reddit

 Email

When I was reading Revenge Wears Prada, I had a strange feeling that I had read it before. At least I had started it, and maybe read the ending? I still am not 100% sure, but if I did read it before, the last two reads were the only time during Another Year of Books that I reread a book. 82 books in, and that’s a pretty good statistic. I know, with 100% confidence, that I have not read this next read. How? This book just came out in summer 2018, and I consulted my list.

Book Read: When Life Gives You Lululemons
Author: Lauren Weisberger
Number of Books Read, 2019 Edition: 30

Emily Charlton is back following her short stint at Runway, working as an image consultant to the stars. Although the number of stars is slowly decreasing. After being fired before even being hired, Emily travels to Greenwich, Connecticut, to visit her friend Miriam. Miriam, a former big time lawyer, is now a stay-at-home mom, navigating Greenwich and its group of mothers, attending lunches and parties on the regular.

While Emily is visiting, Karolina Hartwell, a mutual friend of Emily and Miriam, and wife of a US senator, is arrested for a DUI with her son and other children in the car, despite having half a glass of wine. Her fall from grace is quick and harsh. As she is basically banished to Greenwich, her friends begin to put the pieces back together. 

In a narrative split among the three women, Emily, Miriam, and Karolina band together, to repair an image, save face, and navigate the social circles of Greenwich, CT. Together, they learn three heads are definitely better than one, and with a friendship like theirs, they might just be unstoppable. Add in outrageous parties, surprise visits, revealed secrets, and a surprise ally in Miranda Priestly, and you’ve got When Life Gives You Lululemons. 

While I had finished reading this book, I texted my sister and said “When Life Gives You Lulelemons is 100x better than Revenge Wears Prada.” That is an exact quote - she agreed. I was disappointed with Weisberger when I read Revenge Wears Prada. It made me doubt what the third novel in the series would hold. But Weisberger did not disappoint me here.

Having three main characters in this one worked better than I thought it would when I first picked this up. While each story line intertwined nicely, each character had their own conflict and path. Emily is still entertaining as ever, and is dealing with losing clients to the latest Instagram star. She is also dealing with a looming decision about returning to Runway. Karolina has been seriously screwed over by those closest to her, but all she wants is to raise her son, and maybe get a little revenge along the way. Miriam gave up her career to raise a family, but the lawyer side of her still has the itch, all while dealing with a secretive husband.

You’ll laugh, you’ll root people on, and you will probably roll your eyes occasionally. While When Life Gives You Lulelemons is “A Devil Wears Prada Novel,” it is in no means a sequel. The only continuation is the characters themselves, and even then it is very few beyond Emily and Miranda. If you thought Miranda’s antics were outrageous in The Devil Wears Prada, you’ll be surprised what happens in Greenwich. 

Did I love every single page of this book? No. At times I rolled my eyes at the way grown women talked. Did I enjoy it anyway? Yes. When Life Gives You Lululemons is a good choice when needing to stay inside because it feels like 96 outside (what I am currently doing), or if you’re sitting in a comfy chair with your feet in the water (what I wish I was doing).

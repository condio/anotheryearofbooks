Greetings, readers of Another Year of Books!

It’s been awhile since my last post, so I wanted to provide an update on what’s coming your way soon.

For the past few weeks, since I finished The Two Lives of Lydia Bird, I have been reading whatever I want. Whether that’s a corny romance novel or re-reading a book I read in the past few years, I’ve been doing it. It’s helping pass the time and entertaining me along the way, so I can’t complain.

Now, there are two e-books sitting in my Loans queue from the library and four recently-ordered books from Amazon waiting to be read; sounds like I better get reading!

Here’s a peek of what’s coming to Another Year of Books over the next few months (in no particular order):

  • Attachments by Rainbow Rowell
  • The Jetsetters by Amanda Eyre Ward
  • Sex and Vanity by Kevin Kwan (I couldn’t not buy the newest book by the author of Crazy Rich Asians!)
  • Big Summer by Jennifer Weiner
  • All Adults Here by Emma Straub
  • The Library Book by Susan Orlean

By the time I finish these, I’m sure many more will be added to my “to read” list. Well, back to the books!

Talk to you soon,

Emily
